Rec The Trail

Rec The Trail will give you recommendations for awesome Washington Hikes that fit your hiking style, saving you time looking for what to do on your day off!

Find Out More

What it does?

Rec The Trail parses ratings from Washington Trail Association trip reports and finds you hikes you should do based on prior preferences. New to hiking? We can provide you hike recommendations based on your own preferences! Or completely at random! You choose.
